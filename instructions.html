Instructions

Thanks for participating in this experiment! The data we collect will be used to compare the performance of marking menus and radial menus.

What you have to do

In the next screen, you will see this screen:

The large orange rectangle is the zone where you interact with the page.

At the top you can see a table with some information in it. The most important ones to note are:

  • Trial: The trial you are on / the total number of trials you need to complete. To complete a trial, you need to select a menu item using either a Marking Menu or a Radial Menu. Each trial will take approximately 10 to 15 seconds.
  • Menu Type: The type of menu you are supposed to use. Will be either a Marking Menu or a Radial Menu. Look at the bottom of the page for instructions on how to use Marking Menus or Radial Menus.
  • Please select: The name of the target item you need to select.
  • Item selected: The item you have selected. This field will be blank if you have not selected anything. When you have completed your selection, you will see the item you selected here.

After you have selected an item, and you see your selection appear in Item selected, click the "Next" button on the top right corner of the page to move on to the next trial.



How to use Marking Menus

To open the menu, hold down Left Mouse Button.

Move your cursor to the desired menu item.

Wait for the next level of menu items to appear, if any.

Release Left Mouse Button when your cursor is over the item you want to select.



How to use Radial Menus

To open the menu, click the Right Mouse Button.

Left click on the menu item you want to select.

Wait for the next level of menu items to appear, if any.

Left click on the menu item you want to select.



Once you are familiar with the instructions, and know how to use Marking Menus and Radial Menus, click the "Next" button below.
